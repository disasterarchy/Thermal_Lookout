About Thermal Lookout

An IOT Thermal Imaging Camera

History

After leaving lithium-ion batteries cycling all weekend unattended, Erik Archibald thought to himself it would be great to be able to monitor them remotely. This idea led to the creation of Thermal Lookout which is an internet of things (IOT) enabled thermal imaging camera. The camera is made possible due to the Lepton core from FLIR and the Purethermal 2 board.
